About Jamie

A Lifelong Love for Dogs & the Outdoors

From the moment I could walk, I was walking dogs. Growing up just minutes from the redwoods and open ridgelines of Marin County, I fell in love with the trails — and I can't imagine a better way to share them than with a pack of joyful, tail-wagging companions by my side.

Over the last six years, I've turned that passion into a full-time profession. I've had the privilege of walking hundreds of dogs across Marin — from spunky terriers to laid-back golden retrievers — and I treat every single one like they're my own. To me, a great walk isn't just exercise. It's connection, enrichment, and the simple joy of being outside.

I keep my groups small so every dog gets the attention they deserve. I'm Pet First Aid certified, fully insured, and I send photo updates on every walk so you can see exactly how much fun your pup is having.

  • 🌿
    Nature-first walks — trails, parks, and green spaces, not just sidewalks
  • 🔒
    Safety always — fully insured, Pet First Aid certified, GPS tracking on every walk
  • 📸
    Photo updates — you'll never wonder if your pup is having a good time
  • 👥
    Small groups — max 4 dogs per walk so every dog gets real attention
